Campus Life

Terrace

Was established on 2001

The terrace was a huge room at the complete top of the school. We mostly came here for assemblies and sports practice when it would rain.

Sports Ground

Was established on 1998

The sports ground was always lively. I suppose there was another school there. Children from the neighbouring school would always watch us do sports, laugh, encourage and cheer us on.

Classrooms

Was established on 2001

The classrooms are usually very clean until someone spills a drink or food on the floors. The classrooms are pretty big and have a projector where we sometimes see educational videos from our topic. The students and teachers both control the projector with a computer in the corner of the classroom.

Achievements

Handwriting Competition
Has three positions for handwriting competition

The handwriting competition has separate competitions for two languages- English and Hindi

GK Quizz
Won tin the GK Quiz competition

The Quiz has two rounds; one the enrolling round, the other the finale. To get into the finale round you will have to participate in the first round which is where they give us GK questions we have to answer. If we get more then 8 right, we go into the finale team. Usually three students go into the finals.

What's new

News in RN PODAR SCHOOL

  • School is getting rebuilt. Goal is to complete by 2022??

    R.N.Podar school broken down. The school is going to be rebuilt. Their goal is to finish by 2022. When the COVID pandemic has lessened the students from the school will attend the building nearby. The builders want to make the project 2x bigger than the original school. Builders are planning to give a more modern touch.

  • New Online class

    Due to the COVID Pandemic the school had to shut down before the semester ended. The PTM was held online. The principal thought it was a good idea to have online classes. Online classes started shortly after. There were issues but the school managed.
